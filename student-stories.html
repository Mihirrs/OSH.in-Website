What OSH Families Say

What stands out in Mitushi’s experience is the combination of genuine personalisation and self-motivated learning — two children, each finding their own footing, in a program that moved with them rather than ahead of them.

Suvarna’s family came in sceptical about online learning and left convinced — with a child who enjoyed the process and two working parents who finally had the flexibility they needed.
