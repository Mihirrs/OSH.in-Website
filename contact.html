What Should You Say?

Anything at all. Tell us about your child — their age, where they are in their learning journey, what you are trying to figure out. Or just say hello. We are happy to chat, answer questions, point you in the right direction, or simply listen.

There is no obligation and no pressure — just a real conversation with people who have been through this themselves and genuinely enjoy helping families find their way.

Not sure where to start on the site before reaching out? Our Homeschooling in India guide and NIOS Guide cover the full picture. If you want to understand what the OSH program looks like specifically, start with Our Program.
