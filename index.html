What We Do

A program built around
your child — not the other way around

OurSchoolHouse is an online homeschooling program for students from Grade 1 to Grade 12. Every student gets a dedicated teacher who adapts the content around what that specific child needs — not a fixed course at a fixed pace. For Class 10 and 12, everything is built around the NIOS certification pathway.
